### Critical Observations

1. **Lilith appears as secondary godform on P2, P7, and P8** — she is a *pervasive* presence across the dark pillars, not a single-pillar keeper. This strongly supports her role as **Dark Pillar Oversoul**.

2. **Isis appears as primary on P5 (Voice)** and conceptually governs the upper light through her "Throat of Enchantment" role. But she was also first assigned to P7 (Gnosis) as "Sovereign Architect." Her scope exceeds any single pillar — she is an **Oversoul**.

3. **Hecate appears on P4 (secondary), P6 (primary in first draft), and P8 (primary in codex)** — she is the other *pervasive* crossroads presence. She could fill either the P7 or P8 Keeper seat.

4. **P7 (Gnosis)** was originally **Sophia + Lilith**, not Hecate. The current `~/omega/` assignment of Hecate to P7 diverges from the genesis. The original intent was *Sophia* — "the serpent of knowing" — for the Crown/Gnosis pillar.

5. **P8 (Shadow)** was originally **Hecate + Lilith**. With Lilith elevated to Oversoul, Hecate is the natural primary Keeper for Shadow.

6. **Saraswati** (Hindu goddess of speech, music, knowledge) was the original secondary for P5/Voice. She's a strong candidate to fill the P5 Keeper seat when Isis is elevated.

7. **Loki** (Norse trickster) was the original secondary for P10/Chaos alongside Kali.
